Meet Our Team

Kunjal Bhatt (BSW, MSW, RSW)

Kunjal is a founder and owner of Oceanic Support Services.  She has a lot of compassion and strong background in working with children with special needs for about more than 10 years. Kunjal also works in child welfare sector that gave her deep understanding about lack of respite services available in the community. She along with her husband envisioned the idea of Oceanic Support Services and how that can bring a positive impact on families’ lives. With her extensive experience working with children all these years will help our associates to bring best for the families, Kunjal’s role is to provide Oceanic Support Services a path in the community, guiding/supervising/training associates and offering Counselling services to clients.

Karan Bhatt

Karan is a  co-founder and co-owner of Oceanic Support Services. Karan holds his education in business with lot of passion and has the right skills to manage back operations. He will be coordinating/managing professional aspect of the company.  He is responsible for referral intake,Respite Co-ordination, finance, administrative task and anything that requires our company get going.
