Join Our Team at Oceanic

At Oceanic, we are always on the lookout for passionate and dedicated individuals who are eager to make a difference in the lives of others. If you thrive in challenging situations and have a genuine desire to support families and the most vulnerable populations, we would love to hear from you.

As a respite worker at Oceanic, you will have the opportunity to provide essential in-home support services, giving caregivers much-needed breaks while ensuring their loved ones receive the highest standard of care. Our team members are known for their compassion, reliability, and professionalism, and we are committed to fostering a supportive and rewarding work environment.

We believe that our associates are our greatest asset, and we invest in their ongoing development and well-being. If you are a skilled and qualified individual with a passion for caregiving, we encourage you to apply to join our team. At Oceanic, you will be part of a community dedicated to making a positive impact and improving the quality of life for children and youth across Southwestern Ontario.

If you are interested in becoming a part of our team, please send your resume to info@oceanicsupportservices.com. We look forward to learning more about you and exploring how you can contribute to our mission of providing exceptional respite care.
