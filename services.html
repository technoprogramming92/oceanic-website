Vision

Empowering individuals and families through compassionate care and support, our respite agency envisions a community where every person finds solace, rejuvenation, and renewed strength.

Mission

To provide essential respite services with dignity and respect, supporting individuals and families in their journey towards physical, emotional, and mental well-being through personalized care and professional expertise.

Services

Counselling

We provide expert counselling services addressing family conflict, relationship dynamics, and children's mental health and behavioural challenges. Our approach is designed to support individuals, couples and  families through effective communication, resolution strategies, and tailored therapeutic interventions.

Children’s respite Care

We provide in-home respite services to the children when their family needs a break while ensuring children’s needs are met.  These can include  personal care, meal preparation, companionship and supervision.  Our respite workers are skilled and experienced individuals.  We provide tailored services according to the needs of the family.

Our aim is to help prevent  burnout, reduce stress and allow care givers to focus on their own needs.

Record Keeping

We will keep the records of our associates activities with the family while protecting confidentiality and privacy. These records will always be shared with families on what is happening with their child.
