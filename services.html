Services

Children’s respite Care

We provide in-home respite services to the children when their family needs a break while ensuring children’s needs are met. These can include personal care, meal preparation, companionship and supervision. Our respite workers are skilled and experienced individuals. We provide tailored services according to the needs of the family. Our respite service includes but not limited to,

  • In home
  • In the community
  • One on one support
  • Two on one support
  • Mobility assistance
  • Medication reminders
  • Light housekeeping
  • Personal care
  • Meal preparation
  • Companionship

Counselling

We provide expert counselling services. Our approach is designed to support individuals, couples, and families through effective communication, resolution strategies, and tailored therapeutic interventions.

  • Relationship counselling
  • Postpartum counselling
  • Anxiety, Depression, trauma
  • Behavioural counselling

If you have counselling needs other than the areas mentioned above, feel free to contact us to assess if we can help you.

We work with all major insurances and provide direct billing.
