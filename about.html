Short story about our company

Oceanic Support Services aims to provide respite support and counselling services to families and individuals in London,ON and surrounding areas.

Respite Services are for families who requires break from their daily care giving role to the most vulnerable population and counselling services to individuals, couples and families facing behavioural problems, relationship struggles or any mental health struggle that becomes barrier in their daily life regardless of their age and gender.

Our associates are highly skilled and qualified to provide services with vulnerable sector check and respite training certificate.

We provide respite services to children and youth (Up to 18 years) in their family homes in London, ON and surrounding area. Our counselling services are available to everyone.

What makes us unique?

  • We will be accountable to provide services to you that we have committed.
  • We will make sure that our associates are aware about the need and challenges of your child.
  • We will arrange a meeting with the family and children before hand to gather as much information to assure safety and comfort of everyone. 
  • As an organization, it’s our responsibility to make sure our associates and families are well connected.
  • We will be taking continuous feedback from our associates and families on how can we improve our standards of service.
  • We believe strictly in no hold/restraining a child under any circumstance

Our Core Values

  1. Affection: We foster a culture of warmth and compassion, treating everyone with kindness and empathy, creating a supportive environment where individuals feel valued and cared for.
  2. Respect: We uphold the dignity and worth of each person. We listen actively, communicate openly, and promote a culture of mutual respect in all interactions.
  3. Diversity and Inclusion: We celebrate diversity in all its forms and actively cultivate an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcomed, valued, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives and talents.
  4. Honesty: We conduct ourselves with integrity and transparency, building trust through honest communication and ethical behaviour. We prioritize clarity and accountability in all our actions and decisions.

Meet Our Pioneers

Kunjal Bhatt (BSW, MSW, RSW)

Kunjal is the founder and owner of Oceanic Support Services. She has a lot of compassion and a strong background in working with children with special needs for more than 10 years. Kunjal also works in the child welfare sector, which gave her a deep understanding of the real lack of respite services in the community. She, along with her husband, envisioned the idea of Oceanic Support Services and how it could positively impact families' lives. Kunjal’s role is to provide Oceanic Support Services a path in the community and offer counseling services to clients.
